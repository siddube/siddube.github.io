Serving the right mix

The Balanced plate Cafe is inspired from the philosophy that all foods in proportion provide benefits. We offer fresh, simple and balanced gourmet quality dishes with a focus on nutrition and taste. Our plates offer sumptuous nutrition required to fuel your day as well as leave you feeling satiated.

We offer a wide range of balanced plates to suit almost all eating styles, including cravings, and something indulgent. Our plates offer fresh ingredients, cooked to perfection to ensure the highest quality and nutrition.

We are also happy to customize our plates, where possible, to support your eating styles.

The Team

Prashanth Rao

Prashanth has seventeen years of experience in the IT space, having founded and managed three companies, is a certified serial entrepreneur. Realising that hard work and success meant nothing without good health, he began embraced healthy eating, running and regular exercise as a way of life, getting his health parameters back to normal without any medication! In the last four years Prashanth has competed in multiple Marathons and enjoyed every minute of the training, nutrition and journey. He pursued a Diploma in Fitness and Sports Nutrition from NESTA to further hone his knowledge. He is also a Level 1 Certified Coach in General Fitness Nutrition from Precision Nutrition. He realized his dream of making fitness and nutrition a veritable lifestyle by rolling out Priming Essentials – a personal Fitness Training and Nutrition Coaching consultancy. Passionate about fitness and making a difference in the lives of people, Prashanth has set himself on a journey to educate people on the right fitness and nutrition, and to bring about a holistic and positive change in their lives.

Kalyan Gopalakrishna

Kalyan, a connoisseur of fine food and spirits, has expertly leveraged his natural talents and those acquired, in cooking, blending and creating beautiful food from cuisines across Asia Pacific. Armed with a degree in Business Management and experience of having worked in finance sector, have helped him to successfully blend into the dynamic world of F & B. He has extensively tasted his way across the Asia Pacific region and is known to travel for food & is currently exploring regional cuisines across the country. Being a person who is unmistakably passionate about food, he is considered an authority on Pork & has created The Bangalore Pork Lovers Club on Facebook, where he curates exotic multi course dining experiences for Pork Lovers in Bangalore. He is a member of Rotary International and has served as the President of Rotary Bangalore R. T. Nagar during 2010-2011. He is also a wine and whisky connoisseur & has been the Past President of the Rotarian Wine Fellowship of India.
